MI 2892 Power Master

The most advanced model that offers a number of advanced features, including transient recording and VFD (variable frequency drive) measurements.

  • Accuracy IEC 61000-4-30 Class A 0.1 %
  • Power measurements fully compliant with IEEE 1459 and classic (vector or arithmetic) method
  • Power quality analysis according to EN 50160, IEEE 519
  • Energy (active, apparent, non-active, apparent, generated, consumed)
  • Harmonic and interharmonic analysis up to 50th harmonics, THD and TDD measurements
  • Capturing and recording of power supply events
  • Unbalance, flicker measurement
  • Waveform/inrush displaying, snapshot and recording
  • Inrush currents monitoring and recording
  • Transients recording
  • VFD (variable frequency drive, 5 Hz – 110 Hz), 400 Hz
  • Photovoltaic inverter effciency measurements
  • Recording up to 7 adjustable alarms
  • Temperature measurement
